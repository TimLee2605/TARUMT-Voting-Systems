Rule of Voting

Ensuring a Fair and Transparent Election Process

At TARUMT, we are dedicated to upholding the principles of fairness, integrity, and inclusivity in every election. To maintain the credibility and transparency of the voting process, we have established the following rules:

RULE:
  • Eligibility

  • Ensure you are at least 21 years old and registered as a voter at an Election Commission (EC) office or online.

  • Verify Voter Details

  • Check your voter registration details on the Election Commission's website or by contacting their call center.

  • Polling Station Assignment

  • Confirm your designated polling station on the same website once parliament is dissolved.

Rule
  • Arrive Early

  • Reach the polling station early to avoid long queues. Your IC number will be verified to prevent misuse.

  • Voter Verification

  • Election clerks will check your name and IC, crossing it off the electoral list.

  • Indelible Ink

  • A second officer will apply indelible ink to your finger to prevent double voting.

Rule
  • Mark Your Ballot

  • Mark "X" for your chosen candidate in the voting booth. Ensure the mark stays inside the box to avoid spoiling your vote.

  • Submit Your Vote

  • Fold the ballot paper and place it into the correct ballot box for parliamentary or state seats.
